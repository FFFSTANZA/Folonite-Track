FOLONITE

Smart Asset Management System - Business Solution Overview

Executive Summary

Folonite is a comprehensive asset lifecycle management platform designed for enterprises to catalogue, monitor, audit, and label fixed assets across multiple locations. The system replaces fragmented spreadsheets and manual tracking processes with a centralized, role-based platform that enables facilities, finance, and audit teams to collaborate efficiently while maintaining proper controls and accountability.

The platform delivers measurable business value through reduced asset losses, improved audit compliance, streamlined maintenance workflows, and enhanced operational visibility across the entire asset portfolio.

  • Lack of audit trails raises questions about accountability

Inefficient Maintenance Operations

  • Work orders lack proper documentation and tracking
  • SLA compliance remains difficult to measure and enforce

Operational Silos
