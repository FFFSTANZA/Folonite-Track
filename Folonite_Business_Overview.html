Core Platform Features

Everything You Need to Manage Assets at Scale

1. Asset Registry & Lifecycle Management

The foundation of Folonite is a comprehensive asset registry that captures everything your organization needs to know about each asset:

  • Rich Metadata: Capture purchase dates, warranty periods, PO numbers, serial numbers, specifications, and custom fields
  • Status Tracking: Active, In Maintenance, Retired, Disposed — with full history of status changes
  • Ownership & Location: Clear assignment to departments, properties, and responsible individuals
  • Condition Assessment: Standardized condition ratings with photographic evidence
  • AMC Management: Track maintenance contracts with automated renewal alerts
Business Use Case: Capital Planning

Finance teams use the asset registry to identify upcoming warranty expirations, budget for replacements, and analyze total cost of ownership. The expiry dashboard highlights assets requiring attention in the next 30, 60, or 90 days, enabling proactive rather than reactive capital planning.

2. QR Code System & Mobile Operations

Every asset receives a unique QR code that serves as its digital passport:

One-Click Asset Lookup

Scan any QR code with a mobile device to instantly view asset details, history, and documentation.

Bulk Label Generation

Generate QR code sheets for entire departments or properties in PNG, PDF, or ZIP formats.

Custom Label Sizing

Adjust label dimensions to match your printer hardware and labeling requirements.

Print History Tracking

Prevent duplicate printing and maintain audit records of all label generation.

Business Use Case: Field Operations

Facilities teams conducting building inspections simply scan asset QR codes to verify presence, update condition ratings, and log maintenance needs. What previously required clipboards and data entry now happens in real-time with automatic synchronization.

3. Approval Workflows & Governance

Maintain control over sensitive asset operations with configurable approval chains:

  • New Asset Creation: Route high-value acquisitions for manager or admin approval
  • Asset Modifications: Require sign-off for changes to critical fields like ownership or value
  • Decommissioning: Multi-level approval ensures assets aren't retired without proper authorization
  • Audit Trail: Every approval decision is logged with comments and timestamps

SLA Tracking

Kanban & List Views

Visual workflow management with drag-and-drop status updates.

Asset Linkage

Every ticket links to the specific asset, building complete maintenance history.

6. Reporting & Business Intelligence

Turn asset data into actionable insights:

  • Dashboard Metrics: Real-time visibility into asset counts, values, conditions, and upcoming expirations
  • Operational Reports: Export asset registers, audit summaries, and transaction logs to Excel, PDF, or CSV
  • Department Analytics: Compare asset allocation and utilization across departments
  • Compliance Reports: Pre-formatted reports ready for external auditors and regulators
  • Custom Filters: Slice data by property, department, asset type, date range, and status

Property & Department Scoping

Multi-Location Security

For organizations with multiple facilities, Folonite ensures users only see assets at locations they're authorized for. A facilities manager at the downtown office cannot view or modify assets at the suburban campus, maintaining operational security and simplifying user experience.

Permission Matrix

Beyond role defaults, administrators can grant or restrict access to specific modules:

  • Assets Module: View-only or edit access to asset records
  • Properties Module: Visibility into location directory and assignments
  • QR Codes: Ability to generate and print asset labels
  • Reports: Access to operational and compliance reporting
  • Audit: Participation in and visibility of audit sessions
  • Users Management: Administration of team members and permissions
